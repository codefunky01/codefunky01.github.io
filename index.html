Recipe #1: Classic Chicken Noodle Soup

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 8 servings
Ingredients Quantity
unsalted butter (tablespoon) 2
yellow onion 1/2
garlic cloves 2
celery stalks 2
kosher salt (teaspoon) 1
ground black pepper (teaspoon) 1
chicken broth (cups) 4
chicken breasts 2
carrots 2
fresh thyme (teaspoon) 1
egg noodles (ounces) 8

Step 1: Get ingredients.

Step 2: Cut up veggies and chicken, dump into a pot to boil, along with other ingredients.

Step 3: Serve.

Recipe #2: Classic Turkey Club Sandwich

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 1 servings
Ingredients Quantity
mayo (tablespoon) 1
lettuce 1/2
tomatoes (slice) 2
bread of your choice 2
mustard (tablespoon) 1
turkey (slices) 10
salt and pepper (pinches) 2
pickle 1

Step 1: Get the ingredients.

Step 2: First, slather mayo and mustard onto one side of each bread

Step 3: Second, stack lettuce, tomatoes, turkey in whichever order.

Step 4:Third, sprinkle salt and pepper.

Step 5: Finally, put slice of bread on top of stacked ingredients and serve.

Recipe #3: Classic Chocolate Chip Cookie

  • Time: 95 minutes
  • Serves: 30 servings
Ingredients Quantity
all-purpose flour (cups) 2 1/2
baking soda (teaspoon) 1
fine salt (teaspoon) 1
unsalted butter (tablespoons) 12
packed light brown sugar (cups) 3/4
granulated sugar 2/3
large eggs 2
pure vanilla extract (teaspoon) 1
12-ounce bag chocolate chips 1

Step 1: Get ingredients.

Step 2: Make batter with all ingredients

Step 3: Use either baking sheet or spray cooking oil, than pour and shape batter onto baking pan.

Step 4: Put in a preheated (375 degress, but may differ) oven and bake until golden, but soft in center.

Step 5: When done baking, cool for a minute and serve.
